What is Malware?

Malware is shortened term used for malicious software. Any software that is created for ill intent and is meant harm against user devices, data, or persons, is consisdered malware.

In regards to Malware, if you have ever heard the terms, Virus, Spyware, Trojans, Adware, Ransomware, etc, these are other forms of malware that exist.

Much of the original forms of malware were used as jokes, pranks, or experiments, but overtime, malware is used and created for the sake of profit. A few examples would be intrusive advertisments, stealing of personal and private information, and spam.
