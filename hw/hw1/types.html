What types of Malware are there and what can they do?

Malware is created to cause problems whether it be with the computer or the content on these devices. Here is a list of types of Malware and a brief overview on each sub-type:

  • Adware: Adware is a form of forceful advertising, which can lead to many forms of pop-ups, which are annoying. Adware can also leave a hole for other forms of malware to enter your device.
  • Botnet: Botnets are groups of infected computers, creating a network that can be used by the controller. Often times, the computer users wont even know that their computer is infected.
  • Ransomware: Ransomware. or Scareware, can lock down computers and threaten to delete all information unless the owner pays the attacker.
  • Spyware: Spyware is designed to spy on user, often hiding itself in the background and records information on what sites you visit, credit card numbers, and passwords.
  • Trojan Horse: Trojans disguise themselves usually as helpful softare, that will secretly create an opening for more malware to slip into your computer.
  • Virus: Viruses, like the biological infection agent, sticks to uninfected files and spreads to infect other files. Viruses can get out of control and corrupt important files. Virus files are usually executable files.
  • Worm: Worms are a form of malware that self-replicates so that it is able to spread to other devices, often infecting networks of devices.
