How to Protect Yourself.

The best way to protect yourself from malware would be to make sure you are cauious about what emails you open and what attatchments you may click on. When browsing the internet it is also best to be mindful about what sites you visit, as many suspicous sites exist on the internet.

Another good idea would be to install a good quality antivirus program. Good recommendations could include Malwarebytes, Avast, Norton, and McAfee.
